Morning Lesson
----------------------

This is a catch-up and assessment completion week.  (Some students may have late submissions or re-submissions due for their various ATs.)

A: Ask for and discuss any questions the students may have with:
    - ICTDBS506 - AT2.
    - BSBCRT512 - AT1 or AT3
    - ICTICT517 - AT1

---

A: Students to individually present their final concept for BSBCRT512 - AT1 so they complete BSBCRT512 - AT1.  

NOTE:
- These presentations should be about 5-10 minutes each, and presented to the class or a small group. 
- A couple of points of feedback is required by the teacher and/or the group so the remaining tasks for BSBCRT512 - AT1 can be completed.
