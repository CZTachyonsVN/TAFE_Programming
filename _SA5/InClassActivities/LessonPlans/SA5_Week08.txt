---------------------
Morning Lesson
----------------------

A: Ask for and discuss any questions the students may have with ICTDBS506 - AT1 and AT2.

A:  Ask for and discuss any questions the students may have with BSBCRT512 - AT3

---

I:  Further discussion in relation to creative thinking & originating concepts.

A: With reference to the client project and scenario for ICTDBS506 start to look at the first few tasks and the ideation process outlined in BSBCRT512 - AT1.  Discuss potential options / technologies / techniques that might assist the client in improving their business operations.  Encourage the students to be openly creative with ideas and possible options for the client.

I: Indicate to the group that narrowing down on 'screen and report designs' (as required by ICTDBS506_AT2) as their 'final concept' in BSBCRT512 - AT1, might reduce their overall workload. 

A: Students to break into small groups to brainstorm options for BSBCRT512 - AT1 - Tasks 1.2, 1.3 and 1.4.

A: Individual work on ICTDBS506 - AT1 & AT2, plus BSBCRT512 - AT3, and group work on BSBCRT512 - AT1, with teacher support.
